Welcome Sneaker Heads

This website is all about giving you guys a fighting chance to get the shoe you like without having to enter raffles or any drawings where most of the time you never get chosen. At Wavey Kicks we like to give our customers a fighting chance to snag thoughs fire kicks you always wanted without having to worry about people getting first dibbs on shoes cause they work at the place. How it works is you can either join the live auctions happening now or you can sign up to add a maximum of three shoes you would like to buy to get a notification when that auction is live. Once you enter a live auction the bids starts at 0 and raises increments of $1 untill the hour time limit ends. The last person who throws in that $1 gets the shoe and than the auction continues on until that shoe size is no longer avaliable and there is no more shoes in the inventory. It is as simple as that to get a better fighting chance than getting that email saying you werent selected and nobody wants to see that.
